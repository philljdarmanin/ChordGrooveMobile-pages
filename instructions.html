Getting Started

Chord Groove helps you quickly create chord progressions, match them with strumming grooves, and practice with smooth BPM-based playback.

  • Open the Chords tab
  • Select your key, mode, and chord count
  • Tap Generate to create a progression
  • Save your favourites for later playback

Creating Chord Progressions

Choose your harmony

Select a musical mode like Major (Ionian) or Minor, choose your root note, and decide how many chords you want in the progression.

Tip: Start with 4 chords in Major for easy practice loops.

Building Grooves

Create a strumming pattern

Use the Grooves tab to design your own strumming rhythm. Save common patterns so you can quickly reuse them across different progressions.

  • Down / up stroke timing
  • Accents and pauses
  • Reusable saved grooves

Playback & BPM

Move to the Play tab, select your saved progression and groove, then adjust the BPM slider to match your practice tempo.

  • Slow BPM for learning
  • Medium BPM for timing practice
  • Fast BPM for performance rehearsal

Troubleshooting

  • If playback feels off, lower the BPM and retry
  • Make sure your groove has the correct number of beats
  • Restart the app if audio playback stalls
