Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 2026

Chord Groove respects your privacy. The app does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data or identifiable information from users.

Data Collection

Chord Groove does not collect any personal data. The app does not use analytics tools, advertising services, account login, or third-party trackers.

Saved chord progressions, groove patterns, and app preferences are stored locally on your device only. New Session Studios does not receive, store, or share this information.

Permissions

Chord Groove keeps permissions to a minimum and is designed to work offline.

Android

In the Android release build, Chord Groove does not request internet access, network state access, microphone access, camera access, location access, contacts access, photo/media access, external storage access, foreground service access, overlay access, vibration access, or advertising/tracking identifiers.

Saved chord progressions, groove patterns, and app preferences are stored locally on your device only.

iOS

On iOS, Chord Groove does not request access to the microphone, camera, location, contacts, photos, media library, local network, Bluetooth, or app tracking permissions.

The app does not ask for permission to track you across apps or websites.

Audio Playback

Chord Groove plays audio locally using bundled sound files to help users practise chord progressions and grooves. The app does not record, capture, upload, or transmit audio.

Third-Party Services

Chord Groove does not use third-party services or SDKs that collect, process, or share user data.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us at:

info@chordgroove.app
