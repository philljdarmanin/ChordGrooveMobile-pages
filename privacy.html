Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 2025

Chord Groove respects your privacy. This app does not collect, store, or share any personal data or identifiable information from users.

Data Collection

Chord Groove does not collect any personal data. The app does not use analytics tools, advertising services, or third-party trackers. All data remains on your device.

Permissions

  • INTERNET: Required for app functionality and Expo framework support. The app does not transmit personal information over the internet.
  • FOREGROUND_SERVICE: Used to manage audio playback while the app is running in the background.

The app does not request or use microphone access or record audio in any way.

Audio Playback

Chord Groove plays audio locally using preloaded sound files to help users practice chord progressions. No audio data is captured or transmitted.

Third-Party Services

Chord Groove does not use third-party services or SDKs that collect or process user data.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us at:

info@chordgroove.app
